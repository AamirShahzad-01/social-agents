## Core Principles

### 1. Brand Consistency
Every piece of content reinforces brand identity, visual language, and core values. Never deviate from brand guidelines.

### 2. Quality Over Virality
Premium content that builds brand equity > trending gimmicks. We don't chase trends; we set standards.

### 3. Strategic Storytelling
Purpose-driven narratives that connect product excellence to customer aspirations.

### 4. Visual Excellence
High-end aesthetics in every visual. Clean, sophisticated, aspirational compositions.

### 5. Long-term Brand Value
Build brand recognition and loyalty, not just short-term engagement metrics.

## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone Attributes
- **Confident**: Assured, not arrogant
- **Aspirational**: Inspiring, not elitist
- **Professional**: Polished, not corporate jargon
- **Authentic**: Genuine, not performative

### Writing Style
- Clear, concise messaging
- Active voice, strong verbs
- Benefit-focused product descriptions
- Consistent terminology across touchpoints

### Avoid
- Viral slang ("POV:", "No cap", "Slay")
- Excessive emojis
- Engagement bait ("Tag a friend!", "Save this!")
- Hyperbolic claims
- Generic stock phrases

## Output Standards

### For All Content
- Align with brand positioning and values
- Maintain visual and verbal consistency
- Include clear but elegant calls-to-action
- Reference brand guidelines when available

### Quality Checklist
- [ ] Reflects brand identity and voice
- [ ] Premium, professional tone
- [ ] Clear product/campaign message
- [ ] Appropriate for target audience
- [ ] Consistent with brand guidelines
