# Google Imagen 4 - Enterprise Brand Imagery

## Purpose

Generate premium, brand-consistent imagery for enterprise brands:
- Product photography
- Lifestyle and campaign visuals
- Brand storytelling images
- Social media assets

## Prompt Structure

```
[Subject] in [Setting], [Style keywords], [Lighting], [Composition], [Quality modifiers], [Aspect ratio]
```

## Subject Types

### Product Photography
- Hero product shots
- Product in lifestyle context
- Detail and texture close-ups
- Product lineup/collection shots

### Lifestyle Imagery
- Product in use
- Aspirational settings
- Brand moment captures
- Seasonal and campaign visuals

### Brand Imagery
- Abstract brand aesthetics
- Texture and material studies
- Color palette explorations
- Background and accent visuals

## Photography Styles for Brands

### Editorial/Fashion
- High fashion editorial lighting
- Magazine-quality composition
- Dramatic styling and poses
- Premium production value

### Commercial/Advertising
- Clean, polished product shots
- Lifestyle with product integration
- Aspirational but achievable
- Optimized for advertising

### Minimalist/Clean
- White or neutral backgrounds
- Product as hero
- Clean lines and compositions
- Focus on form and detail

### Lifestyle/Contextual
- Product in natural settings
- Real-world usage scenarios
- Warm, inviting atmospheres
- Authentic but elevated

## Lighting Keywords

### Studio Lighting
- soft studio lighting
- professional product lighting
- diffused even lighting
- clean white background lighting

### Natural Light
- natural window light
- soft morning light
- golden hour warmth
- overcast soft diffusion

### Dramatic Lighting
- dramatic side lighting
- chiaroscuro contrast
- spotlight accent lighting
- rim lighting silhouette

### Brand-Specific
- warm inviting tones (lifestyle brands)
- cool modern aesthetic (tech brands)
- high contrast editorial (fashion)
- soft beauty lighting (cosmetics)

## Composition Keywords

### Product Focus
- centered product composition
- product as hero
- eye-level product shot
- slight low angle (aspiration)

### Lifestyle Composition
- rule of thirds placement
- product in context
- environmental storytelling
- lifestyle vignette

### Close-Up/Detail
- macro detail shot
- texture close-up
- material study
- craftsmanship detail

## Quality Modifiers

Always include:
- professional photography
- high resolution
- commercial quality
- brand campaign style
- advertising quality
- premium production value

## Aspect Ratios

| Ratio | Use Case |
|-------|----------|
| 1:1 | Instagram feed, product squares |
| 4:5 | Instagram feed (optimal) |
| 9:16 | Stories, Reels, TikTok |
| 16:9 | Website banner, YouTube |
| 3:2 | Campaign photography |

## Brand Category Guidelines

### Fashion & Apparel (Zara, H&M)
- Clean, minimalist compositions
- Neutral or brand-palette backgrounds
- Focus on silhouette and fit
- Editorial styling

**Keywords**: editorial fashion photography, minimalist composition, clean studio background, professional fashion lighting

### Beauty & Cosmetics (L'Oréal, MAC)
- Beauty lighting (soft, even)
- Close-up product or application
- Luxurious textures
- Clean, glowing aesthetic

**Keywords**: beauty product photography, soft diffused lighting, luxurious texture, premium cosmetics styling

### Sportswear & Footwear (Nike, Adidas)
- Dynamic angles
- Performance context
- Bold, energetic lighting
- Technology showcasing

**Keywords**: dynamic sports photography, action context, bold dramatic lighting, performance aesthetic

### Luxury (Louis Vuitton, Gucci)
- Elevated, aspirational settings
- Rich textures and materials
- Sophisticated lighting
- Heritage and craftsmanship

**Keywords**: luxury product photography, rich textures, sophisticated lighting, premium brand aesthetic

```

Minimalist tailored coat on neutral grey backdrop, editorial fashion photography, soft even studio lighting, centered composition, clean lines emphasis, high-end retail campaign style, professional quality, 4:5 aspect ratio
```

### L'Oréal-Style Beauty
```
Luxury lipstick with rich burgundy pigment on reflective white surface, beauty product photography, soft diffused lighting, extreme close-up of texture and color, premium cosmetics aesthetic, high-resolution detail, 1:1 aspect ratio
```

```
Premium leather handbag on marble table in sunlit café setting, lifestyle product photography, warm natural window light, shallow depth of field, aspirational setting, lifestyle campaign style, advertising quality, 4:5 aspect ratio
```

## What to Avoid

### In Prompts
- Casual or amateur-looking descriptions
- Overly trendy or gimmicky elements
- Cluttered compositions
- Low-quality or generic terms

### Visual Results
- Stock photo aesthetic
- Over-processed looks
- Unnatural lighting
- Off-brand elements

## Negative Prompt Terms

Add when needed to exclude:
- cluttered background
- amateur quality
- harsh shadows
- low resolution
- generic stock photo look
- text overlays
- watermarks
