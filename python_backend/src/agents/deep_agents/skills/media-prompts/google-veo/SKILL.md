# Google Veo 3.1 - Enterprise Brand Video

## Purpose

Generate premium brand video content:
- Product showcase videos
- Brand campaign films
- Commercial-quality footage
- Lifestyle and aspirational content

## Prompt Structure

```
[Subject and action]. [Setting]. Camera: [movement and angle]. Lighting: [description]. Style: [brand aesthetic]. Audio: [if applicable].
```

## Video Types for Brands

### Product Showcase
- Hero product reveals
- Feature demonstrations
- 360-degree views
- Detail and texture close-ups

### Campaign Films
- Brand story moments
- Lifestyle aspirational
- Seasonal campaign content
- Collaboration reveals

### Commercial Content

- Multi-shot sequences
- Call-to-action moments
- Cross-platform assets

### Professional Movements
- slow smooth dolly in/out

### Framing

- tight detail shots
- wide establishing context

## Example Prompts

### Nike-Style Product Video
```
Athletic running shoe rotating slowly on reflective black surface, showcasing cushioning technology and mesh details. Camera: slow 180-degree orbit at product level, tight framing on shoe. Lighting: dramatic studio lighting with rim light accent, deep shadows for contrast. Style: premium sports commercial, bold athletic aesthetic. Audio: subtle ambient electronic, satisfying material sound.
```

### Zara-Style Fashion Film
```
Model in tailored coat walking slowly through minimalist gallery space with white walls. Camera: smooth tracking shot following at hip level, shallow depth of field. Lighting: soft natural light from large windows, clean even illumination. Style: editorial fashion film, minimalist elegant aesthetic.
```

## Production Standards

### Quality Requirements
- commercial production value
- 4K cinematic quality
- professional color grading
- brand-consistent aesthetic

### Pacing
- deliberate, confident pacing
- moments for impact
- commercial timing standards
- elegant transitions

### Duration
- 5-15 seconds for clips
- Longer for hero content
- TikTok/Reels optimized when needed

## What to Avoid

### In Prompts
- casual or amateur descriptions
- viral trend references
- over-complicated scenarios
- off-brand elements

### Visual Results
- shaky or unstable footage
- harsh or unflattering lighting
- cluttered compositions
- low-quality aesthetic

## Negative Prompt Terms

Add when needed:
- shaky camera
- amateur quality
- harsh shadows
- overexposed
- blurry
- low resolution
