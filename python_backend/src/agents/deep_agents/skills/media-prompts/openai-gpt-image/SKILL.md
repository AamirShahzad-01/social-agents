# OpenAI GPT Image - Enterprise Brand Imagery

## Purpose

Generate premium brand imagery using natural language:
- Product photography
- Campaign visuals
- Lifestyle content
- Brand assets

## Prompt Approach

GPT Image excels with conversational, detailed descriptions:

```
A [subject] in [setting], [style and mood], [lighting], [composition details], [quality level]
```

## Subject Descriptions

### Product Photography
```
A premium [product] on [surface], showcasing [specific details like texture/color/form], presented as hero product shot
```

### Lifestyle Imagery
```
A [person/scene] with [product] in [aspirational setting], capturing [mood/moment], natural and authentic but elevated
```

### Campaign Visuals
```
A [creative concept] for [brand type], featuring [key elements], designed for [campaign purpose]
```

## Style Keywords

### Photography Types
- editorial product photography
- commercial advertising imagery
- lifestyle brand photography
- premium fashion photography
- luxury product still life

### Aesthetic Directions
- clean minimalist aesthetic
- sophisticated luxury feel
- modern premium quality
- aspirational lifestyle
- refined elegant composition

### Brand Categories

**Fashion**: clean, editorial, minimalist, modern silhouette
**Beauty**: soft, glowing, luxurious, delicate
**Sportswear**: dynamic, bold, powerful, energetic
**Luxury**: rich, sophisticated, heritage, refined

## Lighting Instructions

### Studio
- soft professional studio lighting
- clean even illumination
- controlled shadows for dimension
- product-focused lighting setup

### Natural
- warm natural window light
- golden hour soft glow
- diffused daylight
- outdoor lifestyle lighting

### Dramatic
- dramatic side lighting
- rim light accent
- chiaroscuro contrast
- spotlight on product

## Composition Guidelines

### Product Shots
- product centered as hero
- clean uncluttered background
- appropriate negative space
- detail-revealing angles

### Lifestyle Shots
- rule of thirds placement
- environmental context
- shallow depth of field
- aspirational setting

### Campaign Shots
- bold visual impact
- brand-consistent elements
- messaging-ready composition
- platform-optimized framing

## Quality Modifiers

Always include:
- professional photography quality
- high resolution detail
- commercial production value
- brand-appropriate aesthetic
- advertising-grade output

## Aspect Ratios

Specify in prompt:
- "square format" (1:1) - Instagram feed
- "portrait format" (4:5) - Instagram optimal
- "vertical" (9:16) - Stories, TikTok
- "landscape" (16:9) - Website, YouTube
- "widescreen" (21:9) - Hero banners

```
A woman's hand holding a premium coffee cup in a sunlit modern café setting, shallow depth of field with warm morning light streaming through windows, lifestyle brand photography, aspirational yet authentic feel, commercial quality, 4:5 format
```

## What to Avoid

### In Prompts
- casual or amateur descriptions
- trendy internet references
- over-complicated scenes
- off-brand elements
- generic stock descriptions

### Results
- cluttered compositions
- harsh or unflattering lighting
- low-quality aesthetic
- obvious AI artifacts
- text in images (unless specifically needed)

## Negative Instructions

Use natural language to exclude:
- "without any text or watermarks"
- "avoiding cluttered background"
- "no harsh shadows"
- "not over-processed or artificial looking"
