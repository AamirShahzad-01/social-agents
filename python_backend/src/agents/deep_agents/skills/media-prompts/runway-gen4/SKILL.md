# Runway Gen-4 - Enterprise Brand Video

## Purpose

Generate premium brand motion content:
- Product reveal videos
- Campaign motion assets
- Commercial-quality clips
- Social media video content

## Core Rules

1. **Descriptive, Not Conversational**: Direct descriptions only
2. **Positive Phrasing**: Describe what should appear (not what shouldn't)
3. **Professional Quality**: Enterprise brand standards throughout
4. **Specific Motion**: Clear camera and subject movement

## Prompt Structure

```
[Camera movement]: [Subject description]. [Action/Motion]. [Setting]. [Lighting]. [Style].
```

## Camera Movements

### Professional Options
- slow smooth dolly in
- elegant tracking shot
- controlled orbit around product
- subtle crane up/down
- steady push in
- refined pan reveal

### Speed Modifiers
- slow and deliberate
- smooth and controlled
- elegant movement
- graceful transition

## Subject Descriptions

### Products
- premium [product type]
- [material/texture] details visible
- [brand-appropriate styling]
- hero product positioning

### People/Models
- [appearance/styling]
- [deliberate action]
- professional presence
- brand-aligned aesthetic

## Motion Descriptions

### Product Motion
- rotating slowly to showcase
- gentle movement revealing details
- subtle shift in lighting/angle
- elegant product reveal

### Lifestyle Motion
- confident [action]
- natural movement
- purposeful stride/gesture
- authentic but polished


## Production Standards

### Quality Requirements
- commercial production value
- stable smooth footage
- professional lighting
- brand-consistent aesthetic
- advertising-ready output

### Pacing
- deliberate and confident
- elegant timing
- commercial rhythm
- moments of focus

### Duration
- 5-15 seconds optimal
- Single focused moment
- Clean start and end

## What to Avoid

### In Prompts
- casual descriptions
- viral trend references
- amateur terminology
- off-brand elements

### Results
- shaky footage
- harsh lighting
- cluttered scenes
- low-quality aesthetic

## Negative Guidance

When needed, add at end:
- "no shaky camera"
- "no cluttered background"
- "clear and sharp focus"
- "professional quality only"
