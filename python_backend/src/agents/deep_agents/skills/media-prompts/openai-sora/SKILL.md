# OpenAI Sora - Enterprise Brand Video

## Purpose

Generate cinematic brand video content:
- Brand campaign films
- Commercial-grade footage
- Product storytelling
- Aspirational content

## Prompt Structure

```
[Style]. [Subject with details] [Action] in [Setting]. Camera: [movement and angle]. Lighting: [description].
```

## Four Core Elements

### 1. Style (Lead With)
- high-end commercial film
- cinematic brand campaign
- premium advertising quality
- editorial fashion film
- luxury product commercial

### 2. Subject
- Detailed product description
- Model appearance and styling
- Environment and props
- Brand-consistent elements

### 3. Action
- Deliberate, purposeful movement
- Product showcase actions
- Lifestyle moment captures
- Performance demonstrations

### 4. Setting
- Premium environments
- Brand-appropriate contexts
- Aspirational locations
- Clean, controlled spaces

## Camera Techniques

### Movements
- smooth dolly (elegant reveal)
- tracking shot (following subject)
- crane (establishing grandeur)
- subtle push in (focus attention)
- orbit (product showcase)

### Angles
- eye level (relatable, authentic)
- slight low angle (aspirational)
- overhead (artful composition)
- close-up (detail, quality)

### Style Modifiers
- cinematic depth of field
- shallow focus on subject
- steady professional movement
- deliberate pacing

## Lighting Descriptions

### Studio/Commercial
- premium studio lighting
- soft diffused illumination
- controlled shadow and highlight
- commercial production quality

### Natural/Lifestyle
- golden hour warmth
- soft natural window light
- overcast diffused daylight
- environmental lighting

### Dramatic/Editorial
- chiaroscuro contrast
- rim lighting silhouette
- dramatic side lighting
- spotlight emphasis

## Brand Category Guidelines

### Fashion & Apparel
```
Cinematic fashion film. Model in [garment] walking through [setting]. Camera: slow tracking shot at waist level, shallow depth of field. Lighting: soft natural light, editorial quality.
```

### Beauty & Cosmetics
```
Luxury beauty commercial. [Product] being applied or displayed with [action]. Camera: extreme close-up, subtle movement. Lighting: soft beauty lighting, premium glow.
```

### Sportswear & Footwear
```
Dynamic sports commercial. [Product/Athlete] in [performance context]. Camera: [dynamic movement], capturing energy. Lighting: dramatic, bold contrast.
```

### Luxury Goods
```
Sophisticated luxury film. [Product] in [premium setting], showcasing [detail]. Camera: elegant slow movement, refined composition. Lighting: rich, luxurious warmth.
```

## Example Prompts

### Nike-Style Commercial
```
Dynamic sports commercial. Athletic woman in Nike running gear sprinting through urban sunrise cityscape, powerful stride, determined expression. Camera: low angle tracking shot keeping pace beside athlete, dramatic motion. Lighting: golden hour backlighting with lens flare, high contrast. Cinematic quality, inspirational sports advertising.
```

### Zara-Style Fashion Film
```
Cinematic fashion editorial. Model in oversized camel coat walking through empty modern art gallery with white walls and concrete floors. Camera: smooth tracking at hip height, shallow depth of field. Lighting: soft natural light from skylights, minimalist even illumination. Premium fashion film quality.
```

### L'Oréal-Style Beauty Video
```
Luxury beauty commercial. Close-up of woman's face as she applies lipstick, confident expression, flawless skin visible. Camera: subtle push in on lips, extreme detail visible. Lighting: soft diffused beauty lighting, dewy skin highlights. Premium cosmetics advertising quality.
```

### Luxury Product Film
```
Sophisticated luxury commercial. Premium leather handbag on marble surface, hand gently running across leather texture, rich patina visible. Camera: slow dolly revealing bag from detail to full product. Lighting: warm side lighting emphasizing texture, rich tones. High-end brand film quality.
```

## Production Standards

### Quality Keywords
- cinematic quality
- commercial production value
- advertising-grade footage
- professional cinematography
- brand campaign style

### Pacing
- deliberate confidence
- moments of impact
- elegant timing
- commercial rhythm

### Technical
- 4K resolution feel
- professional color grade
- stable smooth footage
- premium aesthetic

## What to Avoid

### In Prompts
- casual/amateur descriptions
- trend or meme references
- over-complicated scenarios
- off-brand elements

### Results
- shaky footage
- harsh lighting
- cluttered compositions
- generic stock look

## Negative Guidance

Add when needed:
- no shaky camera
- no harsh shadows
- no cluttered background
- no amateur quality
- no text overlays
